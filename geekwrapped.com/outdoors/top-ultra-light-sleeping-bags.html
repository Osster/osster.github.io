Top 10 Ultra Light Sleeping Bags

Ready for Adventure?

Ultra light sleeping bags are the must-have accessory for camping, hiking, and other adventures. Lightweight as the name suggests, the right ones are also warm, comfortable, and durable. We've researched over 30 sleeping bags and picked the 10 best for you. Ready for an outdoor adventure? Let's get started!

How To Select Your Ultra Light Sleeping Bag

Sleeping bags come in a wide range of styles, materials, and sizes. Different conditions call for different types of sleeping bags. As a result, knowing what to look for when shopping for lightweight sleeping bags can be tricky. Here's what features to consider when shopping.

Unfolded Size

This is the size of the bag, completely unfolded. It determines if you?ll fit in, so keep an eye on this. A sleeping bag that's too large will be unwieldy, while one that's too small won?t work.

Folded Size

How big the sleeping bag is when compressed will speak to its overall portability. Look for a bag that takes up a smaller space when compressed, as it will be easier to move around and store.

Weight

The weight of the bag is another indicator of how easy it is to take with you. Ideally, lightweight sleeping bags should be exactly that - lightweight. We picked only light bags for the choices below.

Material

Sleeping bags come in a wide variety of materials. Some are useful for warmer climates and others are made for cold weather. Nylon is a great, light material and sometimes is mixed with down.

Water Resistant

The sleeping bag you choose should have some degree of water resistance to it. You never know when you might be stuck in the rain, snow, or some other high humidity environment.

Temperature Rating

This factor determines what temperature range that the bag is rated for. Usually related to cold weather conditions, it's the temperature limit at which the sleeping bag will keep you warm.
